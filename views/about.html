About Us

Vision

To harbour ourselves as an incubation haven and translate to an ultimate innovation destination by developing a symbolic relationship between students and professionals entrepreneurs, mentors, corporates and venture capitalists.

Mission

To create an umbrella under which our students nurture their ideas convert them to strong entrepreneurial ventures.

From the Principal's Desk

The contemporary business era demands a larger focus and commitment on entrepreneurial initiatives, especially in a country like India and it is vital for job creation, economic growth and solving societal problems. It also reflects upon a society’s capacity for boldness and risk-taking & creativity, as there is a strong positive association among entrepreneurship, economic growth and innovation.

DJS E-cell has been influential in triggering awareness, interest, desire and sustained action towards building the entrepreneurial spirit required among the students in a significant way. It has been highly successful in providing an ecosystem to the young entrepreneurs in terms of mentorship, technical assistance and networking. It is a catalyst that constantly promotes the entrepreneurial spirit, largely prevalent in our country “. I take this opportunity to congratulate all our E-Cell committee members for their efforts and also thank them for their sustained commitment and hard work. I believe that their efforts will certainly help shape the future of many of our young countrymen, who will be future job providers, rather than being just job seekers.

I wish everyone all well and pray for their continued success.

Regards and good wishes.
